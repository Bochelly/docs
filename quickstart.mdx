## Overview
This section will guide you through the process of integrating Gleef into your website and starting your first A/B testing experiment on wording.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Integrate our JS snippet">
    Our snippet needs to be integrated in your website in order to let us experiment wordings.
    
    <Warning>
      The snippet should be integrated in the whole site in order to manage success events being followed by Gleef.
    </Warning>

    ```html
    <script src="https://cdn.gleef.io/gleef.js"></script>
    <script>
      Gleef.init({
        apiKey: 'YOUR_API_KEY'
      });
    </script>
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get credentials from Gleef">
    Gleef manages access to the extension. You need to ask for an account before being able to use Gleef.
    Please follow the link: [sign up](https://gleef.eu/signup)
    <Info>You can ask for an account only if your company is already using Gleef. You want to become customer, email us at: contact@gleef.eu</Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the Extension">
    In the registration email you've received you have the link to download the extension.
    You can also find it on the following link: [Gleef extension](https://gleef.eu/extension)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch & Manage experiments">
    Once you're all set up, you can now create new experiments as well as manage experiments (start, pause, delete etc.).
  </Step>
</Steps>
